HOW IT WORKS

A live CCTV camera films real traffic on a Jamaican road. You watch the feed, predict how many vehicles will pass, and the AI does the counting. Simple.

  1. 01
    Watch the Live Feed
    A real camera streams traffic in real time — cars, buses, trucks, bikes. Study the flow before a round opens.
  2. 02
    A Round Opens
    The admin starts a timed window — usually 30 to 90 seconds. A countdown appears and betting opens. Don't miss it.
  3. 03
    Place Your Prediction
    Two ways to bet:
    Exact Count Guess the exact number of vehicles. High risk, best payout.
    Over / Under Predict above or below a set number. Easier, lower reward.
  4. 04
    AI Counts Every Vehicle
    When the round closes, computer vision automatically tracks and counts every vehicle that crossed the camera line. No manual counting, no bias.
  5. 05
    Results & Payouts
    Your prediction is compared to the AI count. The closer you are, the better your payout. Exact match = highest multiplier. Check your balance after each round.
Rounds run on a schedule. Stay on the feed — the next one starts any time.

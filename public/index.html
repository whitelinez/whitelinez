HOW IT WORKS

A live CCTV camera films real traffic on a Jamaican road. Watch the feed, guess how many vehicles will pass, and the AI does the counting. Closer = more points.

  1. 01
    Watch the Live Feed
    A real camera streams traffic in real time. Study the flow — how busy is it? How fast are vehicles moving? Use that to inform your guess.
  2. 02
    A Match Opens
    The admin starts a timed match — 1, 3, or 5 minutes. Pick your window and submit your guess before time runs out.
  3. 03
    Submit Your Guess
    Type in how many vehicles you think will pass during your chosen window. Exact match scores the most points. Close guess still earns partial points.
  4. 04
    AI Counts Every Vehicle
    Computer vision tracks and counts every vehicle crossing the camera line in real time. No manual counting, no bias.
  5. 05
    Score & Leaderboard
    Your guess is compared to the AI count. The closer you are, the more points you earn. Build your score across multiple matches and climb the leaderboard.
Matches run on a schedule. Stay on the feed — the next one starts any time.
